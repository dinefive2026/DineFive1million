Our mission

Food should be affordable for everyone

Dine Five was created with a simple mission: make fresh restaurant meals accessible to everyone. By partnering with local restaurants and community supporters, we help families, students, seniors, and individuals enjoy quality meals at an affordable price.

Affordable

Every meal starts at one fair price — $5.99 — so a good meal never depends on a big budget.

Community

Neighbors helping neighbors. Donated meals keep local kitchens busy and tables full.

Fresh food

Cooked to order by real restaurants — not reheated, not factory-made. Just fresh.

Accessible

One tap in the app. Available to anyone — and free for those who need a hand.

Recipient assistance

Support for those who need it most

Through the Dine Five community program, eligible individuals can access donated meals directly through the app. Users may claim up to one donated meal per day when available.

  • Quiet and dignified

    No long lines or paperwork — claim a meal privately, right from your phone.

  • One meal a day

    Eligible users can claim up to one donated meal per day, whenever meals are available.

  • Real restaurant food

    The same fresh, made-to-order meals everyone else enjoys — never an afterthought.
