Account Deletion & Data Safety

We respect your privacy and rights over your data. Follow the steps to request permanent deletion of your Dine Five account and associated records.

What will be permanently deleted

Your profile info (name, email, phone number), active meal subscriptions, saved delivery locations, and rewards balance will be completely and permanently wiped.

Information we legally retain

For legal, financial reporting, tax audits, and compliance purposes, anonymized details of past transactions (meal purchases and donations) will be kept in read-only vaults.

Processing Timeline

Once requested, your account will be immediately deactivated. Full sanitization across all backups, database servers, and third-party tools will be finalized within 7 business days.
